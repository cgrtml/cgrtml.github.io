Welcome: what I write about

This is the start of my writing space. I am an AI and machine-learning engineer and researcher, and the Co-Founder and CTO of Hezarfen LLC. Most of my days are spent at the intersection of building real AI products and thinking about how to make them safe and explainable.

Here I plan to write about the things I keep coming back to: trustworthy and explainable AI, safe Chain-of-Thought reasoning for autonomous systems, interpretable machine learning, and the practical lessons of shipping AI in high-stakes domains. Expect a mix of research notes, engineering write-ups, and the occasional opinion on where the field is heading.

Thanks for reading. If something here is useful, or wrong, I would genuinely like to hear from you — you can reach me from the contact page.
