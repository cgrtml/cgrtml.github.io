About

I am a machine-learning engineer and researcher, and the CTO of Hezarfen LLC. My work sits at the intersection of explainable AI, AI safety, and applied large-language-model systems. I focus on making advanced models trustworthy: grounded, auditable, and safe enough to deploy in high-stakes settings such as healthcare and regulated finance.

As an IEEE Senior Member and a reviewer for the IEEE SMC International Conference, I contribute to the research community while shipping production systems — including retrieval-augmented generation platforms with near-zero hallucination targets and an explainable security layer for LLMs.
